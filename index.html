Forest Engineer

What is a Forest Engeneer?

A forest engineer is a professional who applies engineering principles and techniques to the management, conservation, and sustainable use of forest resources.There are several transferable skills from a forest engineer to a software engineer.

Problem-Solving

Both forest engineers and software engineers need to be able to analyze complex problems and come up with creative solutions to solve them.

Attention to Detail

Forest engineers and software engineers must pay close attention to detail when designing, implementing and testing their solutions.

Analytical Thinking

Both professions require the ability to analyze data and make data-driven decisions

Communication

Forest engineers and software engineers often work on teams and need to be able to explain their work and ideas to others
